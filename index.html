Three Card Tarot Spread

The Fool

This card represents new beginnings, fresh starts, and new opportunities. It can also represent innocence, naivety, and taking risks.

The Magician

This card represents creativity, manifestation, and personal power. It can also represent new skills, new knowledge, and new projects.

The High Priestess

This card represents intuition, subconscious wisdom, and the hidden mysteries of the universe. It can also represent secrets, silence, and introspection.
